About Us

A Story of Love and Hurt.

My beloved daughter, Brisa, left this world too soon, driven to despair by the insidious grip of depression. Her story is one of tragedy, but it's also the driving force behind our mission. As an extraordinary individual, Brisa excelled in both mathematics and art. She ranked second in her high school, earned a scholarship to Ohio State University, and studied computer science engineering. Yet, her true passion lay in art, where she poured her heart and soul into expressing her mental battles and her unique perspective on the world. Her journey with depression led to episodes so severe that hospitalization became necessary. Despite the hardships, her future seemed bright.

About Colibri Foundation

Founded in 2023, Colibri Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and empowering young individuals. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where young minds can grow, learn, and flourish. We believe in the power of education, community engagement, and individual development to transform lives.

At Colibri Foundation, we offer a range of programs including educational workshops, mentorship opportunities, and community service projects. Each initiative is designed to inspire confidence, foster creativity, and encourage a sense of community among our participants.
