Our Vision and Mission

Our vision for Colibri Foundation is to envision a world where every young soul can embrace life without the heavy burden of suicidal thoughts and depression. Our mission is to guide them toward mental well-being through art, community, and compassion.

My beloved daughter, Brisa, left this world too soon, driven to despair by the insidious grip of depression. Her story is one of tragedy, but it's also the driving force behind our mission. As an extraordinary individual, Brisa excelled in both mathematics and art. She ranked second in her high school, earned a scholarship to Ohio State University, and studied computer science engineering. Yet, her true passion lay in art, where she poured her heart and soul into expressing her mental battles and her unique perspective on the world. Her journey with depression led to episodes so severe that hospitalization became necessary. Despite the hardships, her future seemed bright.

Key Innitiatives

We are determined to turn our vision into a reality. Colibri Foundation will be more than just a foundation; it will be a sanctuary where young hearts can find solace and support. Our initiatives include:

  • Art as a Healing Tool: Art is a potent form of expression and healing. Brisa's art, filled with her passion and struggles, will be at the heart of our programs. Proceeds from art sales will directly fund our mental health initiatives.

  • Creating a Supportive Community: We aim to create a supportive, nurturing community where young individuals can find hope, healing, and strength through shared experiences.

  • Guiding Youths towards mental well-being: WWe're collaborating with mental health experts and organizations to provide essential resources, counseling, and guidance to those who need it most.
